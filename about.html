About Selene Sahari

Selene Sahari is an interdisciplinary creator whose work weaves together systems architecture, Sovereign Systems Psychology™, and visual storytelling. Raised on the campus of the China Academy of Art, she absorbed a visual language shaped by structure, symbolism, and spatial rhythm — foundations that continue to inform her work.

Her portfolio moves across continents and disciplines — from software engineering and AI ethics to photographic narratives grounded in presence, silence, and symbolic refusal.

Through DreamWeaver AI, she builds tools for inner clarity — designing systems not only for enterprises but for the inner world. Her current project, The Sovereign Path, is both a visual journey and an evolving theory of authorship beyond visibility.

Ongoing Focus

Selene continues to explore how women reclaim narrative space — not through spectacle, but through structure, design, and deliberate authorship. She believes sovereignty is a design system, and clarity is a form of power.
