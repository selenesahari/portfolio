About Selene Sahari

Selene Sahari is an interdisciplinary creator whose work weaves together systems architecture, Sovereign Systems Psychology™, and visual storytelling. Her portfolio moves across continents and disciplines — from software engineering and AI ethics to photographic narratives grounded in presence, silence, and symbolic refusal.

Through DreamWeaver AI, she builds tools for inner clarity — designing systems not only for enterprises but for the inner world. Her current project, The Sovereign Path, is both a visual journey and an evolving theory of authorship beyond visibility.

Milestones

  • 2024–2025: Earned UC Berkeley Professional Certificate in AI/ML (Top 5% of cohort)
  • 2025: Founded DreamWeaver AI – focused on Sovereign Systems Psychology™
  • 2024–2025: Created "The Sovereign Path" photo essay across Morocco, France, Spain, China, Australia, Canada, and the U.S.
  • 2025: Participated in NVIDIA GTC Hackathon (AI for Identity)
  • 2025: Forthcoming presentation at SciDataCon 2025 (part of International Data Week, Brisbane)

Ongoing Focus

Selene continues to explore how women reclaim narrative space — not through spectacle, but through structure, design, and deliberate authorship. She believes sovereignty is a design system, and clarity is a form of power.
