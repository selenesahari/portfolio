The Sovereign Path (2024–2025)

A cinematic photo essay exploring authorship, presence, and psychological sovereignty through architectural symbolism and spatial storytelling.

  • Submitted to: ND Awards 2025, International Photography Awards (IPA) 2025
  • Visual storytelling across: Morocco, Asia, Europe, and North America

This ongoing series challenges inherited scripts of femininity and visibility. Through framing, gesture, and atmosphere, The Sovereign Path redefines what it means to move through space unperformed — not as object, but as author.

“Selene stands before Moroccan tilework in red — poised, composed, and uncontained.”
“Crossing the Sahara at dusk — goats in motion, sovereignty in stillness.”
“Amid calligraphy and light — authorship without noise.”
