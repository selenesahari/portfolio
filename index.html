Selene Sahari

Interdisciplinary Creator · Visual Artist · Staff Software Engineer · Founder, DreamWeaver AI

Selene Sahari is a narrative visual artist whose work explores presence, sovereignty, and self-authorship through image. Her visual language blends global textures with symbolic solitude, creating spaces where a woman arrives not to be seen — but to see.

With roots in systems engineering and introspective storytelling, her practice investigates how women navigate environments historically shaped to contain them. Her work is not performative. It is declarative.

The Sovereign Path (2024–2025)

A cinematic photo essay exploring authorship, presence, and psychological sovereignty through architectural symbolism and spatial storytelling. Submitted to ND Awards 2025, IPA 2025. Shot across Morocco, Asia, Europe, and North America.
