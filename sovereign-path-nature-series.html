The Sovereign Path: A Visual Rewilding

These three photographs—Liberation, Mosaic Grace, and Shadow & Silk—were submitted to the 2025 SUGi x NAVA Photography Contest under the theme of rewilding. But rather than documenting wilderness, this series reframes rewilding as an internal return: to presence, authorship, and elemental connection.

Whether set in the dunes of Morocco, among ancient mosaic courtyards, or beside a cobalt sea wall, each portrait becomes a study in sovereignty—where fashion meets nature, and gesture becomes signal.
