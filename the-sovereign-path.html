The Sovereign Path

The Sovereign Path is an ongoing visual narrative exploring what it means to move through the world without performing for it. Spanning Morocco, Asia, Europe, and North America, the series follows a solitary woman reclaiming space, identity, and authorship through presence alone.

Structured in three symbolic arcs — Root & Refusal, Power & Poise, and Transcendence & Signal — the project uses self-portraiture and environmental composition to map a quiet, interior sovereignty.

Each frame becomes an act of remembrance and refusal — a visual language for women who no longer seek permission to exist fully in their power.
