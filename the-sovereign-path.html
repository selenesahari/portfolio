The Sovereign Path

Women Photograph Project Grants 2025

This series unfolds in three arcs — Root & Refusal, Power & Poise, and Transcendence & Signal — tracing how sovereignty is enacted through real spaces shaped by empire, architecture, and code. Each image forms a visual stanza in a living documentary of presence.
